I'm a PhD student at SFI ML-LABS, Dublin City University and am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Noel O'Connor and Dr. Annalina Caputo. My research focus on modelling multi-type user behaviour using Graph Neural Networks and efficient Transformer architectures for Recommender Systems. My primary research interests are on Large Language Models and Recommender Systems.
